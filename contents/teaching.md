## 1. Advanced Programming Languages

- **Course Type:** Required Core Course  
- **Target Grade:** Freshman Year (1st Year Undergraduate)  

### Course Outline  
1. **Introduction to Programming Paradigms**  
   - Procedural Programming  
   - Object-Oriented Programming  
   - Functional Programming  
   - Logic Programming  

2. **Advanced Language Features**  
   - Generics and Templates  
   - Exception Handling  
   - Concurrency and Parallelism  

3. **Language Design and Implementation**  
   - Syntax and Semantics  
   - Compiler and Interpreter Basics  
   - Memory Management and Garbage Collection  

4. **Practical Programming Techniques**  
   - Design Patterns  
   - Code Optimization  
   - Debugging and Testing

## 2. Principles of Quantum Communication

- **Course Type:** Elective Course  
- **Target Grade:** Junior Year (3rd Year Undergraduate)  

### Course Outline  
1. **Fundamentals of Quantum Mechanics**  
   - Quantum States and Qubits  
   - Superposition and Entanglement  
   - Measurement Postulates  

2. **Quantum Communication Protocols**  
   - Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)  
   - Quantum Teleportation  
   - Quantum Dense Coding  

3. **Quantum Channels and Noise**  
   - Quantum Noise Models  
   - Error Correction in Quantum Communication  

4. **Applications and Future Directions**  
   - Quantum Cryptography  
   - Quantum Networks  
   - Challenges and Advances in Quantum Communication Technology
